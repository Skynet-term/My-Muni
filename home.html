How We Met

We met in a specific time in our lives when we both learned to love ourselves. It just started out as us having fun. Then it slowly built into something beautiful.

Where We Went

We took a big step at one point and decided to move in with each other. At the time I was regularly sneaking up to her room to be with her. Our love was a secret from those who didn't need to know. Eventually we got an apartment together and then bought a house. One day when Sara was too busy with her new job she couldn't turn and say what she does to Mathias everyday, so she grabbed his hand and handed him a note saying to get to work safe and come back home safely. It was at that moment that Mathias knew he wanted to be with her forever.
